1) MESSAGING NOTIFICATIONS (DM)
TRIGGERS:
- New message in a DM thread

RECIPIENTS:
- All participants in that thread EXCEPT the sender

BEHAVIOR:
- Create notification type: "message_new"
- entityType: "thread"
- entityId: threadId
- Title: "New message"
- Body: "<SenderName>: <short snippet>"
- URL: /messages?thread=<threadId> (or your actual route)

DELIVERABLES:
- Hook notification creation into the message send endpoint (backend) after message save.
- Ensure recipients only include thread participants.
- Confirm unread badge increments and clicking opens the correct thread.

------------------------------------------------------------
2) JOB NOTIFICATIONS
TRIGGERS (ONLY these — keep it tight):
A) Job assigned to a technician (crew assignment add)
B) Job unassigned from a technician (crew assignment remove)
C) Job schedule time/date changed (if scheduling exists)
D) Job status changed to key statuses only (ex: "in_progress", "on_hold", "canceled", "completed") — do NOT notify on minor edits

RECIPIENTS:
- Technicians affected (added/removed tech) for A/B
- All assigned crew for C/D (only those assigned to that job)
- Owner/Admin and Supervisor may also receive C/D if they have job access OR are global-access roles in your RBAC.

IMPORTANT:
- Do NOT notify other technicians not assigned to the job.
- Do NOT notify about jobs a user cannot access.

NOTIFICATION DETAILS:
- type examples: "job_assigned", "job_unassigned", "job_schedule_changed", "job_status_changed"
- entityType: "job"
- entityId: jobId
- Title examples:
  - "Assigned to job"
  - "Removed from job"
  - "Schedule updated"
  - "Job status updated"
- Body should include: Client name (if available) + job title
- URL: /jobs/<jobId> (or open Job Details modal route)

DELIVERABLES:
- Implement backend hooks where these events occur:
  - Crew assignment endpoint(s)
  - Schedule update endpoint (if exists)
  - Status update endpoint
- Ensure notifications are created after DB update succeeds.
- Ensure Jobs UI bell deep-links to the job correctly.

- entityType: "estimate"
- entityId: estimateId
- Title examples:
  - "Estimate created"
  - "Estimate updated"
  - "Estimate approved"
  - "Estimate converted to job"
