“Allowing access for Google OAuth or email user” then later

“Authentication failed: no session or user”

WebSocket connects, receives {type:'auth', userId: ...}, then WS closes

This is classic session cookie not being sent / not being persisted, usually due to CORS + credentials, SameSite/Secure cookie flags, and/or missing trust proxy when running behind Replit/https.

We must fix this properly (not by bypassing auth), and also make WebSocket auth consistent.

Do NOT accept a raw userId without proof.
