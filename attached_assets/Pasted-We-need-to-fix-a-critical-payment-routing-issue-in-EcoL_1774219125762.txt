We need to fix a critical payment-routing issue in EcoLogic.

CRITICAL BUG
Online invoice payments are currently going to EcoLogic’s Stripe/bank account instead of the contractor/company’s connected Stripe account.

That is wrong.

GOAL
When a contractor/company gets paid through an online invoice in EcoLogic, the money must go to THAT contractor/company’s connected Stripe account / bank account — not mine.

CURRENT INTENDED PRODUCT LOGIC
- Each company/contractor should connect their own Stripe account
- If they have NOT connected Stripe yet, the app should route them to the Stripe payouts/connect area in Customize so they can connect it
- Once connected, online invoice payments must be processed into that contractor/company’s Stripe destination, not EcoLogic’s default account

IMPORTANT
- This is a real money-routing fix
- Do NOT do a fake frontend-only change
- Do NOT leave any path where invoice payments silently go to EcoLogic instead of the contractor
- Preserve the existing “connect Stripe first” gating if it already exists
- We need the correct Stripe Connect / payout architecture enforced as source of truth

REQUIRED BEHAVIOR
1. Company has connected Stripe:
   - customer pays online invoice
   - payment is routed to that company’s connected Stripe account
   - contractor receives the funds in their Stripe-connected payout flow

2. Company has NOT connected Stripe:
   - they should NOT be able to route online invoice money into EcoLogic’s account
   - app should block/paywall that specific payment flow appropriately
   - app should send them to the Stripe payouts/connect setup in Customize (or the correct connect flow already in app)

3. There should be no fallback where invoice payments default to EcoLogic’s Stripe account unless that is part of an intentional platform-fee architecture and the contractor’s funds are still correctly routed via Stripe Connect
