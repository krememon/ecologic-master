🧱 1. TOP SCOREBOARD (BIG NUMBERS)

At top of Payments page, add a horizontal stats card with:

This Month → total payments received this month

Still Owed → sum of remaining balances on all unpaid + partial invoices

Paid Today → sum of payments recorded today

Overdue → count of invoices where:
today > job_scheduled_date AND balance > 0

These must update live whenever a payment is recorded.

📋 2. PAYMENT FEED (RECEIPT STYLE)

Each row shows:

Customer name

Invoice number

Amount

Status badge (PAID, PARTIAL, UNPAID, OVERDUE)

Payment method if paid (Cash, Check, Card)

Timestamp or Due label

Example layout:

Jennifer Pellegrino
Invoice #1023
$350 PAID (Card)
Today • 2:41 PM

John Smith
Invoice #1041
$1,200 UNPAID
Due Yesterday

Color rules:

Green → Paid

Yellow → Partial

Gray → Unpaid

Red → Overdue
