


 Headlining Features:
- **Unified Job & Subcontractor Management Dashboard:** A centralized hub where contractors can manage all jobs, subcontractors, client info, and communication in one place, providing real-time updates and status tracking.
  
- **Smart Scheduling & Dispatching:** An intelligent scheduling system that considers subcontractor availability, project deadlines, and resource allocation, minimizing conflicts and delays.

- **AI-Powered Client Insights:** An integrated AI tool that analyzes client notes to predict needs, preferences, or even potential issues, enabling personalized service and better client relationships.

- **Automated Workflow & Reminders:** Automated task assignments, reminders for deadlines, follow-ups, and payment due dates to keep projects on track.

**Chosen Headliner (Example):**  
**"The All-in-One Trade Contractor Management Hub"** — emphasizing simplicity and comprehensive control.


Additional Features 
1. **Project Templates & Checklists:** Predefined templates for common job types to ensure consistency and efficiency.
2. **Digital Contract & Quote Builder:** Create, send, and sign contracts or quotes directly within the app.
3. **Invoicing & Payment Tracking:** Generate invoices, track payments, and send reminders.
4. **Document Storage & Sharing:** Upload and organize permits, blueprints, receipts, and other documents.
5. **Time Tracking & Job Logs:** Track hours worked per job or employee for accurate billing.
6. **GPS & Location Tracking:** Track job site locations and employee whereabouts for better logistics.
7. **Expense Management:** Log and categorize expenses related to each project.
8. **Performance Analytics:** Dashboard showing project profitability, employee performance, and other KPIs.
9. **Customer Feedback & Reviews:** Send automated review requests to clients post-project.
10. **Integration with Accounting Software:** Sync with QuickBooks, Xero, or similar platforms.
11. **Mobile App Access:** On-the-go access for field workers and managers.
12. **Customizable Notifications:** Push notifications for important updates, deadlines, or messages.
13. **Safety & Compliance Checklists:** Ensure safety protocols are followed and documented.
14. **Referral & Lead Management:** Track referrals and generate new leads from satisfied clients.

---

### Step 3: Prioritize Features
Focus on features that deliver immediate value and are feasible for your initial development phase. For example:

- **Must-have for MVP:** Subcontract management, chat system, client notes, invoicing.
- **Next-phase features:** Scheduling, document sharing, mobile access, analytics.
