Hide “Billing & Subscription” from Technician Settings (hidden-not-disabled, no empty state)

CURRENT
- Technician Settings screen shows “Billing & Subscription”
- It displays “No Company Found” and a “Choose Plan” button
- Technicians should never manage billing or subscription

GOAL
Remove Billing & Subscription section from Technician role entirely:
- Tech should not see this card at all
- No “No Company Found” empty state on tech settings
- Billing & Subscription remains available only to Owner/Admin (or whoever manages subscription)
- Mobile-first UI stays clean
