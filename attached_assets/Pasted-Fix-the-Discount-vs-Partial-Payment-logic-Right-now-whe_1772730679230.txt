Fix the Discount vs Partial Payment logic. Right now, when a discount is applied and the customer pays the discounted total, EcoLogic is incorrectly labeling/tracking the payment as Partial and showing “still owed” — even though it should be fully paid after discount.

The bug

Example: Original invoice $700. Discount applied so total due becomes $600. Customer pays $600.
EcoLogic currently treats this as partial and says $100 still owed. That’s wrong.

Goal

A discount is not a partial payment. If the customer pays the final discounted amount due, the invoice should be marked Paid in Full (not Partial), and owed should be $0.

Required correct behavior

Discounts must reduce the invoice’s amount due / total due (the “source of truth” due amount).

Payment status should be calculated against the discounted total due, not the original subtotal.

If amountPaid >= discountedTotalDue, then:

invoice status = Paid

owed = $0.00

do NOT show “Partial” badge anywhere

Only label as “Partial” if amountPaid < discountedTotalDue.

What to inspect and fix

Please find where the system determines:

invoice “amount due”

“still owed” calculation

status labels like Partial / Paid / etc.

payment success screen owed logic

Likely places:

backend invoice/payment status calculation

storage update when recording payment

receipt/summary calculations

any UI badges derived from invoice fields

Data model direction

Make sure the invoice record has a clear “final total due” after discount, such as:

total (final amount after discount)

or amountDue computed from subtotal - discount
(Use the existing schema fields and conventions — but the key point is: status math must use the discounted due amount.)

Implementation requirements

Do not hack this just in the UI.

Fix the source of truth calculation so:

owed amounts are correct

status is correct

badges are correct

payment success screen is correct

receipts are correct

Ensure the fix works for:

full payments with discount (should be Paid)

partial payments with discount (should be Partial until fully paid)

no discount (existing behavior unchanged)

Testing cases (must pass)

Subtotal $700, no discount → pay $700 → Paid, owed $0 (no regression)
