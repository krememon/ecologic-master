Polish “Add New Contractor” modal spacing (remove excessive white space, mobile-first clean layout)

CURRENT
- “Add New Contractor” modal has large empty white space above the form
- Fields start too low on the screen, making the modal feel awkward and unfinished
- Overall spacing looks sloppy on iPhone-sized view

GOAL
Make the modal layout look tight, clean, and professional:
- Reduce unnecessary top padding/empty space
- Bring the form fields higher (closer to the title)
- Maintain comfortable spacing between fields (not cramped)
- Keep it perfectly mobile-first (iPhone view priority)
- No functionality changes, only layout polish
- No console errors
