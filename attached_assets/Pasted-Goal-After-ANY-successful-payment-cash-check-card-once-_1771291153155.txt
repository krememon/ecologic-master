Goal: After ANY successful payment (cash/check/card), once the payment completion signature is saved, EcoLogic should automatically email the customer a receipt email with the exact same invoice PDF we already generate manually for that job (the one shown in the “Generate Invoice PDF” modal). Do NOT generate a different “confirmation invoice” layout. Attach the real PDF.

Requirements (must follow)

Always require signature after payment (remove the Customize toggle / settings dependency).

Remove “Printed Name” field from the signature modal + API (signature is just drawing).

Email triggers only after signature is successfully saved (not immediately after payment).

Email must include the exact existing generated invoice PDF (reuse the same PDF generator/function/route used by “Generate Invoice PDF” for that job/invoice).

Do not send duplicate emails if the signature endpoint is retried (idempotent).

payments.receiptEmailSentAt TIMESTAMP NULL

GET /api/invoices/:invoiceId/pdf or

Generate a Buffer (or fetch the existing Buffer) and attach it to Resend:

filename example: Invoice_${invoice.invoiceNumber}.pdf

set receiptEmailSentAt = now()
